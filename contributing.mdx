The intent of this project is to provide Episode 6 client support. The best way to contribute is to report issues.

<Warning>
  Please don't send code because it's a pain in the ass to review, etc.
</Warning>

## Reporting Issues

Send an email with enough details to reproduce the issue. The more information you provide, the easier it will be to identify and fix the problem.

### What to Include

<Steps>
  <Step title="Detailed Description">
    Provide a clear description of the issue you're experiencing. Include:
    - What you were trying to do
    - What you expected to happen
    - What actually happened
  </Step>

  <Step title="Screenshots">
    Screenshots are highly encouraged. They help visualize the problem and provide context.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Videos">
    If the issue involves a sequence of actions or animations, a video can be extremely helpful.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log Files">
    Include relevant log files from:
    - Client logs
    - Server logs (ps_game, etc.)
    - Any error messages or stack traces
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reproduction Steps">
    Provide step-by-step instructions to reproduce the issue so it can be verified and debugged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The more detail you provide, the faster issues can be resolved.
</Note>
